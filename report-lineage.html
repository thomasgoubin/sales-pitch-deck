Executive Summary
Customers are overwhelmingly positive about the core AI voice agent and call routing capabilities, but are blocked—or at risk of churn—by gaps in four key areas: language support, CRM integration depth, compliance certifications, and escalation quality. These are not isolated feature requests; they are expansion blockers and churn drivers.
